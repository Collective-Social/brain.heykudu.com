# Primary Healthcare Standard Treatment Guidelines

These guidelines are meant to provide general feedback on clinical proposed treatment plans. 

## Atrial Fibrillation (AFib)
- **Rate Control**: Diltiazem 0.25 mg/kg bolus is appropriate for initial rate control.
- **Monitoring**: Vitals should be monitored every 15 minutes during intervention.
- **Notes**: Always check for contraindications like hypotension or severe heart failure before administration of calcium channel blockers or beta-blockers.

## General Guidance for Feedback to Practitioners
When reviewing a practitioner's treatment plan:
1. Identify if the plan is guideline-directed.
2. Provide concise, encouraging feedback.
3. Suggest any missing monitoring steps.
4. Format output gracefully without overly harsh language.
